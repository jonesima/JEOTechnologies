About Me

I'm a passionate Full-Stack Developer with over 7 years of experience building web applications that solve real-world problems. My journey in tech began when I built my first website at 25, and since then, I've been obsessed with creating digital experiences that are both functional and beautiful.

What drives me is the challenge of turning complex ideas into simple, intuitive interfaces. I believe that great software should not only work well but also delight its users. My approach combines meticulous attention to detail with a focus on performance and scalability.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me contributing to open-source projects, mentoring junior developers, or exploring new technologies. I'm particularly excited about the potential of serverless architectures and progressive web apps to transform how we build for the web.

Fun fact: I can solve a Rubik's cube in under 2 minutes, which I think reflects my love for solving puzzles—whether they're made of plastic or code!
